    • ⋙ Cats

    I've had many cats throughout my life, and they've always been a joy to live with. They're quirky, independent, loving and cuddly. The ones I've loved the most were the ones that I've owned as an adult and thus had to scoop their poop myself...

    Chunky

    My spirit animal. He's starting to get up there in years, and he will leave a hole in my heart when he crosses the rainbow bridge. He is my constant companion, but he's also a dirty thief. He stole a bag of Funyuns † from me once and ate half the bag before I could wrangle it back from him.

    Nougat

    Chunky and Nougat were the first cats I owned out of college. While we only had Nougat a short while, he was the most mellow cat I've ever had, and very loving. He had lots of fun weird quirks like getting trapped in paper grocery bags and climbing on top of the fridge to take a nap.

    Snickers

    By far one of the "cat"-iest cats I've had. She's extremely weird (can only pick her up a certain way, don't touch her paws, etc), and slinks around the house. She's also probably one of the fattest cats I've had. She's probably as heavy as my youngest son. She will nap in the bathtub (no water), eat plastic bags, and chew through all of my electronics cords. She's pretty funny when I give her some catnip though.
